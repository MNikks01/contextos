# ContextOS — HIRING

Hire behind revenue; first hires reclaim founder time on the critical path. See BUSINESS_STRATEGY.md §5.

## Stage 0 — Solo founder ($0 MRR)
You do everything: full-stack, AI, product, GTM, support. Leverage AI coding agents heavily (this repo is the spec they build against — D-011). Don't hire yet; bandwidth comes from focus + tooling, not headcount.

## Stage 1 — First engineer (~$0–10K MRR)
- **Role:** Full-stack engineer (TypeScript/Next/Node), often a contractor first.
- **Why:** owns feature delivery so you can sell + talk to customers.
- **Profile:** product-minded generalist comfortable with AI APIs; ships fast; high autonomy.
- **First 90 days:** own V1 collaboration + observability features.

## Stage 2 — AI engineer (~$10–25K MRR)
- **Role:** AI/ML engineer focused on retrieval/agents/evals/COGS.
- **Why:** RAG-over-code quality and cost are the moat and the margin; needs an owner.
- **Profile:** strong on RAG, embeddings, evals, prompt/context engineering, cost optimization; pragmatic (we don't train models — D-001).
- **First 90 days:** own the eval harness + retrieval quality + cost-per-task.

## Stage 3 — DevRel + Founding sales (~$25–50K MRR)
- **DevRel:** content, docs, community, demos, OSS stewardship. Unusually high leverage for dev tools — often before a salesperson.
- **Founding sales/GTM:** first repeatable Team/Enterprise motion once inbound proves out; handles SSO-era enterprise pipeline.

## Stage 4 — Scale (>$50K MRR)
Infra/SRE (reliability, K8s, enterprise/VPC), Design (polish), Support/Success (retention/expansion), more eng (per roadmap).

## Hiring principles
- Small + senior as long as possible; generalists early, specialists as the surface grows.
- Hire for the bottleneck on the critical path (build → then sell).
- Culture: reliability-obsessed, customer-close, build-in-public friendly.
- Equity-generous for early hires; remote-first.
- Use contractors to test a role before a full-time hire.

## Org sketch at ~$50K MRR
```
Founder (CEO/Product) ── Eng1 (full-stack) ── AI Eng ── DevRel ── Founding Sales
                                              └── (SRE/Design/Support as needed)
```

## What NOT to hire early
- A manager (you're too small).
- A salesperson before PLG/inbound proves the motion.
- Multiple junior engineers (coordination cost > output at this stage).
